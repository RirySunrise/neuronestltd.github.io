The Space

Designed with purpose, built with love

Every corner was designed with neurodivergent children in mind, with input from therapists and families who knew what was missing.

A space that works for every child

Neuronest was designed from scratch around the real sensory, motor, and emotional needs of neurodivergent children, with every detail deliberately chosen.

Built for how neurodivergent children actually play

Sensory Swings, Ninja Warrior Swing and Trapeze Bars

Vestibular input that helps children regulate, focus, and feel grounded. Different swing types support different sensory needs.

Crash Pit and Climbing Wall

Safe, supervised proprioceptive exploration. Children who need heavy input to regulate will find this space essential.

Quiet Room

A calm, low-stimulation space for children who need to step away and regulate. No food, no shoes, no noise.

Sensory Wall

Tactile panels, mirrors, and light elements to explore touch, reflection, and cause and effect at their own pace.

Creative Play Pods

Art, craft, and imaginative play in a structured, contained space. Designed for focus and connection.

Learning Room

A structured activity space with sensory toys, puzzles, and tools. Used for the Preschool Readiness Program and directed play.

Therapy Suites

Fully equipped rooms for occupational therapy and speech therapy, used by our partner practitioners.

Visual Schedules and Signage

Clear, consistent visual cues throughout the space help children understand where they are and what comes next.

Parent Waiting Area

Stay as close or as far as you need. Our waiting area keeps you nearby without requiring you to be on the floor at all times.
