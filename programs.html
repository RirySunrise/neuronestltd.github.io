Open Play
Walk in. No appointment needed.

A safe, sensory friendly space where your child can explore freely. Stay beside your child, sit nearby and watch, or step back and let our trained staff take care.

  • Weekdays: KES 1,000 per child / 3 hour session.
  • Weekends: KES 1,500 per child, 3 hour session. Additional hours at KES 500 each.
  • Adults: Accompanying adults enter free.
  • Payment: M PESA Paybill 542542, bank transfer or card. No cash.

Preschool Readiness Program
Preparing your child for school, through play.

A structured program for neurodivergent children who need focused support before entering mainstream school. Therapy and school preparation are brought together in one place, around each child's individual goals.

Our specialist team works together daily so that progress across communication, regulation, motor skills, and social readiness happens in a coordinated way. Families are kept involved throughout so that learning extends beyond the program itself.

  • Who it is for: Children aged 3 to 8 who need support before entering mainstream school.
  • What is covered: Sensory regulation, communication, motor development, social skills, and school readiness routines, all embedded into a structured daily program.
  • Parent involvement: Regular progress updates so development continues at home and beyond.
  • Each child's program is tailored individually. Contact us to find out if this is the right fit for your child.

Therapy Sessions
Professional therapy, right here on site.

Therapy suites are available on site for occupational therapy and speech therapy, provided by independent qualified practitioners. Therapy can happen in the same visit as play.

Our therapy rates are set at the minimal viable rate to keep services as accessible as possible. For current pricing, please contact us directly and we will share full details and connect you with the right practitioner.
