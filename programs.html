Programs and Services

Everything your child needs, in one place

Open Play
Walk in. No appointment needed.

A safe, sensory friendly space where your child can explore freely. You can stay beside your child, sit nearby and watch, or step back and let our trained staff take care.

  • Weekdays: KES 1,000 per child. Currently unlimited time.
  • Weekends: KES 1,500 per child, 3 hour session. Additional hours at KES 500 each.
  • Adults: Accompanying adults enter free.
  • Payment: M PESA Paybill 542542, bank transfer or card. No cash.

Preschool Readiness Program
Preparing your child for school, through play.

A structured program designed for neurodivergent children preparing to enter a school environment. Led by our specialist teacher and supported by our on site therapists, it builds the foundational skills children need through play based methods that work for how neurodivergent children actually learn.

  • Who it is for: Children aged 2 to 6 preparing for preschool or primary school.
  • Structure: Small groups with a rotating specialist model and individual attention.
  • Led by: Our lead special needs teacher with OT and speech therapy support.
  • Enquire for current cohort availability and fees.

Therapy Sessions
Professional therapy, right here on site.
