Open Play at Neuronest

Bring your little ones to explore, move, and play in a safe and inclusive environment designed to meet every child's sensory needs. Open Play is the perfect way to enjoy our space without any commitment — just freedom, fun, and the right environment for your child to thrive.

Important Notes
  • Open Play gives access to the playground only. Therapy suites are separate and bookable independently.
  • Parental supervision is not mandatory. You are welcome to stay and watch or leave your child in the care of our trained staff.
  • All bags and shoes must be placed in the cubbies at the front desk before entering the play area.
  • Food, drinks, and gum are not permitted inside the playground. Please use the designated eating area.
  • Please remove any sharp objects and accessories before entering the play space.
  • Bullying, inappropriate language, and disruptive behaviour will not be tolerated at Neuronest.
  • A signed waiver is required before your first visit. Our team will walk you through this at the front desk.

Neuronest Play Rules

Where play meets respect and every child feels safe. Thank you for helping us keep the space fun, clean, and welcoming for every family.

01

Shoes Off, Socks On

Place all shoes in the designated area at the front. Grip socks keep the space clean and safe — bring your own or purchase a pair from us.

Socks are required for entry.
02

No Food in the Play Area

To keep our space clean and allergy friendly, all food and drinks stay in the reception or break area. The play zones are food free, drink free, and gum free.

03

Choose Kindness

Play gently, share the space, and use caring words. Everyone has more fun when we take turns and treat one another with patience and respect.

Kind play keeps the space happy.
04

Quiet Corner is a Calm Space

Our quiet corner is designed for rest, regulation, and deep breaths. Soft voices, slow movements, and calm bodies. Shoes, toys, and food stay outside.

A perfect place to reset.
05

Equipment Stays Together

All toys and equipment are built for hands-on fun. Keep pieces together, and if anything feels loose, let our team know straight away.

06

Swings and Climbing

Hold on with both hands, take turns, and move with awareness. One rider at a time on swings. Our team is always nearby to support safe play.

07

Care for the Space

Please tidy up after your session, let staff know about spills, and help us keep the environment fresh for every family who comes after you.

08

Everyone Belongs Here

We celebrate children of all abilities, learning styles, and personalities. Kindness and open hearts make Neuronest a place where every child feels valued.

Play kindly. Laugh loudly. Explore safely.
Neuronest is a play-at-your-own-risk environment. A signed waiver is required before entry. Following staff guidance keeps everyone protected and comfortable. Safety is our shared responsibility.

Thank you for helping make Neuronest a warm, inclusive, and joyful place for every child and every family.
